## 4 cron-learnings logged this arc

1. **Concurrent `git add` race** — pathspec form `git commit -m '...' -- <paths>` is the only race-free shape in parallel-agent waves
2. **Browser-extension globals are not our problem** — `window.sunWeb` (Sunmi POS) closed as `not planned` with diagnosis, not "guard our own code"
3. **GitHub auto-close trailer format** — each `#N` needs its own keyword on its own line; slash/space-separated `Closes #N #N` only auto-closes the first
4. **`emitEvent` fire-and-forget** trips vitest unhandled-rejection guard if `prisma.automationRule.findMany` isn't stubbed in tests that POST event-emitting routes
