Core Capabilities

Everything your sales team needs

From first contact to closed deal — Globus CRM covers the entire customer lifecycle with intelligent automation.

Agent Assignment

Assign sales agents to leads and contacts with one click. Bulk-assign multiple leads at once. Round-robin distribution built into the seed.

Agent-wise Reports

Performance leaderboard ranking every agent by revenue, deals won, win rate, tasks, calls, and emails sent.

Detailed Reports + Download

8 metric types, date range filters, chart + table views. Export any report as PDF or CSV with one click.

Auto Email Reports

Schedule daily, weekly, or monthly reports delivered via email as PDF or CSV. Manage recipients and toggle schedules from the UI.

AI Lead Scoring

Machine learning engine scores every contact 1-100 based on engagement, company profile, and deal history. Runs on cron every 10 minutes.

Drag-Drop Pipeline

Kanban board with real-time Socket.io sync. Drag deals between stages — all connected users see changes instantly.
